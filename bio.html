Hi, my name is Karan. I am the co-founder of TensorParc.



In 1990, I got my first PC and began coding. In 1998, I started one of the first online stores in Germany, Geschenk24.com, which was acquired by Metro in 2001.

In 2015, I founded CodeDoor, a platform empowering people to learn and teach coding.

Since 2019, I have been on a mission to bring space-based studies to German classrooms. We help grades 6 to 12 conduct earth-based experiments that are also performed on the ISS.

Since 2022, my team and I have been running the Creators Program—coding camps that help students build anything they want.

Sometimes, when venture capitalists need insight into startups and their founders, they call me for advice.

My curiosity has led me to hold lectures about my findings at the University of Giessen and the Handelshochschule in Leipzig. I’ve advised many large corporations as well.

I strongly believe that solutions to many of today’s challenges can be found outside the current societal mainstream. The writings of Bahá'u'lláh, for example, I find particularly thought-provoking.

I play around with science topics in general. If you want to talk about or play basketball, send me an email.
